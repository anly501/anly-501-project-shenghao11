Introduction

My topic is about the natural growth rate of the population in each country of the world. Population issues have been the subject of discussion for decades. Overpopulation puts pressure on natural resources, exacerbates conflict and war, and leads to water scarcity and malnutrition, but shrinking populations also pose many problems. Population growth rates vary from region to region. Some countries, such as South Korea, Japan, Syria and Italy, have seen their populations decline, whether due to high immigration rates or declining birth rates. What factors influence the growth/decline of a country's population. I will collect data on economic crime, unemployment rates, etc. from different countries and compare it to population growth rates to determine which factors cause natural population growth/decline and predict future demographic changes in each country.
